Who We Are

More than a meal —
it's an experience

Nestled in Gulberg, Faisalabad, Sober Cuisine was born from one belief: great food should never be ordinary. We blend smoky charcoal BBQ, time-honoured Desi recipes, and crowd-favourite Fast Food under one roof.

Every dish is made fresh, every order with care. Whether it's a family dinner, a quick lunch, or a late-night craving — we've got a plate with your name on it.

🔥 The smoke never lies. Come taste the difference.
